# Financial Analysis of Intertek Group Plc

## Overview
This project presents a detailed financial analysis and valuation of Intertek Group Plc, a global leader in quality assurance and testing services. The study evaluates the company’s financial performance, profitability, liquidity, efficiency, and investment potential through ratio analysis and valuation techniques.

## Objectives
- Analyze the financial performance of Intertek Group Plc
- Evaluate profitability, liquidity, gearing, and efficiency ratios
- Compare Intertek with industry competitors
- Assess stock market performance and investment prospects
- Estimate company valuation using financial models

## Topics Covered
- Financial Ratio Analysis
- Capital Structure & Debt Analysis
- Dividend Policy
- Profitability & Liquidity Analysis
- Stock Performance & Beta Analysis
- Valuation Models:
  - Dividend Discount Model (DDM)
  - Earnings & Investment Model
  - Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF)

## Methodology
The project uses:
- Annual reports and financial statements
- Ratio analysis
- Peer company comparison
- FTSE 100 benchmark analysis
- Financial valuation models

## Key Findings
- Intertek demonstrates strong profitability and operational efficiency
- The company maintains a balanced capital structure with manageable debt
- Long-term growth and investment prospects remain positive
- Market performance indicates strong shareholder returns despite theoretical overvaluation
